Ye / Kanye West

Ye, commonly known as Kanye West, is an American rapper, record producer, entrepreneur, and fashion designer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest and most influential hip hop musicians of all time, as well as one of the greatest musicians of his generation.

"Everyone's always telling you to be humble. When was the last time someone told you to be great?"

West's musical career is defined by frequent stylistic shifts and different musical approaches. Over time, West has explored a variety of music genres encompassing soul, baroque-pop, stadium rock, electro, house-music, indie rock, synth-pop, progressive rock, industrial, punk and gospel.

One of the world's best-selling music artists, with over 160 million records sold, West has won 24 Grammy Awards, the joint tenth-most of all time, and the joint-most Grammy awards of any rapper along with Jay-Z.

"I'm not comfortable with comfort. I'm only comfortable when I'm in a place where I'm constantly learning and growing."

West's approach to record production is consistently rich, nuanced and thought-provoking. His productions are akin to musical compositions, bearing an ornate style with more depth typical of hip hop artists while making use of an ear for hooks. He incorporates live instruments, manipulated vocal samples and dramatic arrangements to supplement his beats.

"I just sat back and decided that I'm gonna be the best-dressed rapper in the game"

Early in his career, West made clear his interest in fashion and desire to work in the clothing design industry. West's Yeezy shoe line is considered one of the most influential sneaker brands in the world. Adidas Yeezy is a fashion collaboration between German company Adidas and Kanye West.

The collaboration has become notable for its high-end limited edition colorways and general releases offered by the Yeezy Boost sneakers lineup. The collaboration has also produced shirts, jackets, track pants, socks, slides, women's shoes, lingerie and slippers.

"Most people are slowed down by the perception of themselves. If you’re taught you can’t do anything, you won’t do anything. I was taught I can do everything."
